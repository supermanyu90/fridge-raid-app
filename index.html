Assistance & FAQ

How does this app work?

Simply enter the ingredients you need to use up, your supplementary grocery budget, and your day's schedule intensity. We'll generate a tailored meal plan, a smart grocery list, and a prep to-do list.

What are 'Substitutions'?

If a generated grocery item is too expensive, click "Substitute" to swap it for a budget-friendly alternative. Your budget tracker will instantly update!

Why is the progress bar changing colors?

The progress bar shows your budget utilization. Green means you're safe, orange means you're approaching your limit, and red means you've exceeded it.
